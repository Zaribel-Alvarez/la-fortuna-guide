Where to Stay in La Fortuna: Best Areas & Hotels

Updated 2026 | Find your perfect home in the rainforest.

Choosing where to stay in La Fortuna depends on two things: your budget and whether you have a rental car. The area is divided into three main zones, each offering a completely different experience.

1. La Fortuna Downtown (Budget & Social)

The town center is the best place for solo travelers, backpackers, and those relying on public transport. You'll be steps away from the best sodas, supermarkets, and bus stations.

Recommended for:

People without cars, budget travelers, and foodies who want to be near the town's park.

2. The Road to the Volcano (Luxury & Resorts)

This is the "Golden Mile" of La Fortuna. Along this road, you will find the world-famous hot spring resorts. Most hotels here have a **direct view of the Arenal Volcano** and are surrounded by lush jungle.

This area is perfect for couples on their honeymoon or families looking for a high-end experience. You will definitely need a car or a taxi to get to town from here.

3. El Castillo (Nature & Privacy)

About 25 minutes from town, El Castillo is a quiet village with the best views of Lake Arenal and the volcano's "dark side." It's the place to stay if you want to be completely disconnected and surrounded by nature.
