Ultimate Packing List for La Fortuna

Don't forget these essentials for the rainforest.

Planning a trip to Arenal? The weather in La Fortuna can be unpredictable. You might have a sunny morning and a tropical downpour in the afternoon. Here is what you must bring.

1. The "Must-Haves"

  • Light Rain Jacket: Even in the dry season, rain is common. A breathable poncho or jacket is vital.
  • Hiking Shoes/Boots: Trails at the volcano and waterfall can be muddy and slippery. Good traction is key.
  • Quick-Dry Clothing: Avoid denim. Use synthetic fabrics that dry fast in high humidity.

2. For the Adventures

If you are visiting the hot springs or the waterfall, don't forget:

  • At least two swimsuits (they take a long time to dry).
  • Water shoes (highly recommended for the river rocks).
  • Biodegradable insect repellent and sunscreen.
